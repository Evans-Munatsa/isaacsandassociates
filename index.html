Welcome to Isaacs & Associates

We Always Fight For Your Justice to Win

At our firm, we focus on building long-lasting and trusting relationships with our clients. We take the time to fully understand your business, ensuring that we can provide the support you need to maintain legal compliance in various areas.

Why not choose one law firm to handle all your needs—from labor law and debt collection to company law and beyond! We're here to offer you comprehensive legal solutions tailored to your unique requirements.

Here are some of the areas Isaacs & Associates cover:

Practice Areas

Debt collection

Debt collection is the process of pursuing payments from individuals or businesses that have outstanding debt.

Labour law

Labor law is a body of regulations and legal principles that govern the relationship between employers, employees, and labor unions

Divorces

Whether it is by consent or litigous, we will assist in mediation, negotiations, attending to court, attending to the family advocate and drafting of various documents required.

Estates

This involves the process of administering the assets and liabilities of a deceased person, through the legal procedure known as "estate administration".

Commercial law

Commercial law governs the legal aspects of business transactions and commercial activities.

Civil litigation

The legal process through which individuals or entities resolve non-criminal disputes through the court system.

HR functions

HR functions involve managing and supporting the workforce within an organization.
